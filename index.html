our story

Visit Matchbox, one of Australias leading independent kitchenware & cookware retailers.
Enjoy an engaging in-store experience and chat to knowledgeable staff about how to improve your cooking skills. With over 25 stores in Victoria, QLD & WA, Matchbox stocks a range of specialist kitchenware offers and brands. These include: Anolon, Maxwell & Williams, KitchenAid, Circulon, Mundial, Tefal by Jamie Oliver, Global, Cuisinart, Essteele, Wusthoff, Riedel, Woll, Silit, Essteele, Scanpan and more. As brands vary across stores, please check with your local Matchbox store for specific brands or products.
