Tableware

When it comes to taking your home-cooked creation from the kitchen to the dining table, Matchbox has everything you need to present it with pride. With so many stunning tableware ranges to choose from Matchbox has selected the hottest products from brands like Maxwell & Williams, Salt & Pepper, Peer Sorensen and Ecology. No matter what your personal styles there is a tableware collection for you. See more

When it comes to dinnerware Matchbox boasts a broad range of stoneware, porcelain and fine bone china. Dinner plates, side plates, bowls and so much more featuring bold patterns that are updated with the latest trends, neutral tones or simple yet elegant white, there is a dinnerware collection to match your own unique style.

Don't just dish it up, plate it up proudly on some beautiful servingware from Matchbox. With porcelain, glass or wood to choose from we have all your food presentation needs covered from cheese boards & platters to cake stands & plates.

Complete the setting with a brand new cutlery set. A beautiful set of cutlery from Maxwell & Williams or Stanley Rogers is just what you need to add that final flourish to your table setting. Most of our cutlery has matching serving utensils available to ensure the look is completed from start to finish.

The best thing about the tableware collection at Matchbox is that just about everything can go straight in the dishwasher for clean up, to be sure check the manufacturers care instructions for your chosen products or contact us for more information.
